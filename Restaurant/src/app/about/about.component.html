Our History

Started in 2020, Developer's Restaurant quickly established itself as a culinary icon par excellence in Dhaka. With its unique brand of world foods that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in Dhaka. Featuring four of the best three-star Michelin chefs in the world, you never know what will arrive on your plate the next time you visit us.

The restaurant traces its humble beginnings to The Frying Pan, a successful chain started by our CEO, Mr. Sk Samiur Rahman that featured for the first time the world's best cuisines in a pan.
